Background

I'm Sophia Lin, a part-time student at BCIT in Vancouver with a focus on UX design as my career path. I'm drawn to design problems that come from genuine frustration — the kind where you keep running into a wall and think,
why doesn't this exist yet?

My approach is grounded and systems-oriented. I think through edge cases before I get excited about solutions, and I'd rather find a real problem than a polished answer to the wrong question. That mindset shapes how I research, design, and communicate.

Skills

  • UX Research & User Interviews
  • Wireframing & Prototyping
  • HTML & CSS Development
  • Information Architecture
  • Presentation Design & Delivery
  • Critical Analysis & Problem Framing

Interests

Outside of design work, I'm into music, fashion, food, baking, and dance. A lot of my design ideas come from personal frustrations — like wishing you could have more detailed filters when shopping for clothes online, or wanting a better way to track new music releases from my favourite artists. I care deeply about the details and the user experience of everyday products, and I think that passion shows in my work.

I believe the best products get built by people who actually feel the problem.
